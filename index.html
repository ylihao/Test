Welcome to the iProperty Group

The iProperty Group is a leading online advertising business serving the real estate industry and is owner of Asia’s No. 1 network of property websites under the iProperty.com umbrella brand.

Established in 2003, the Group operates property portals and delivers innovative advertising solutions to its advertisers in the markets of Malaysia, Hong Kong, Indonesia and Singapore with investments in India and Philippines. The Group recently launched the first regional commercial and industrial property website, CommercialAsia.com.

With further expansion planned, the iProperty Group is continuously working to capitalise on its market-leading positions and the rapidly growing online property advertising market throughout the region.
